Glacier Energy Services in Aberdeen provides fast response and turnaround services for sectors such as agricultural, plant, commercial vehicles, distilleries, marine and industry from Inverness to Dundee for the repair, remanufacture and new supply of a complete range of heat exchangers including radiators, oil coolers, intercoolers and heaters. We aim to save you money against main dealer costs and all our products are fully tested and come with a full guarantee.

Glacier's Heat Exchanger Workshop is in the heart of Aberdeen Harbour and our team consists of many highly trained heat exchanger repair technicians, some with more than 30 years experience.

We understand that unexpected repairs need to be dealt with quickly, and our technical experts are able to offer a range of cost effective solutions from the oldest vintage tractor to the latest modern day equipment. We offer a free diagnosis service and can organise collection and return delivery to your location.
